About Domestic Abuse

Domestic abuse includes any attempt by one person in a marriage or intimate relationship to dominate and control the other. It extends beyond partner relationships and can also be perpetrated by parents.

These behaviors can manifest in various forms, including physical, emotional, economic, and psychological abuse. Lack of knowledge about domestic abuse can create confusion, making it challenging for individuals to understand and recognize the signs. This platform aims to provide information, support, and resources to empower individuals to navigate through these challenging situations.
